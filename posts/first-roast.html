My First Time in a Galaxy Far, Far Away

June 8, 2026

I had heard about Star Wars my whole life — the lightsabers, the Force, the "I am your father" moment — but I had never actually watched it. That changed one rainy afternoon when a friend finally put on A New Hope and said, "Just trust me."

The opening crawl hit and I was hooked before the first Star Destroyer even appeared on screen. Luke staring at the twin suns on Tatooine felt like the most relatable thing in the galaxy: a kid who wanted something bigger than the farm he was stuck on.

By the time the Death Star trench run started, I understood why people have been obsessed with this franchise for nearly fifty years. I stayed up way too late that night and watched The Empire Strikes Back immediately after. No regrets.
