Essential Star Wars Media

June 9, 2026

The movies are the foundation, but Star Wars has sprawled into TV, books, comics, and games. The original trilogy remains non-negotiable — everything else builds on those three films in some way. The prequels fill in the fall of the Republic; the sequels ask what happens after the Empire falls.

On the small screen, The Clone Wars deepens Anakin and Obi-Wan's relationship across seven seasons. Rebels bridges the gap to the original trilogy. Andor is the most mature, grounded storytelling the franchise has ever produced — no Jedi required. The Mandalorian is a fun entry point if you want something episodic and action-heavy.

For readers, Timothy Zahn's Thrawn novels are legendary among fans, and the new canon has plenty of great comics and young-adult series. For gamers, Knights of the Old Republic still holds up. Start with what excites you most — the franchise is big enough to meet you wherever you land.
